Chartwell Chamber Site Plan

Site Name

Chartwell Chamber of Commerce. This name reflects the locale's identity and indicates the chamber's purpose to support local businesses.

Site Purpose

The site will serve three primary purposes:

  • To provide information about local businesses and services available in Chartwell.
  • To promote community events and networking opportunities among members and the public.
  • To promote skill development by hosting workshops once a month.

Scenarios

Here are some common questions from site visitors:

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

Selected colors for the website:

  • #0056b3 - Used for headings and accents.
  • #f4f4f4 - Used for the background color of the site.

Typography

Selected fonts for the website:

  • Roboto - Used for body text.
  • Verdana - Used for headings.
